What We do?

Flower House has proudly been serving the community of Zapopan and surrounding areas for more than twenty five years. While others have come and gone, The Flower House continues to thrive. Over the years, we have built a reputation for combining quality and unique style with competitive low prices. This formula has earned us long standing relationships with our customers and allowed us to build new ones everyday. Our philosophy is simple. Provide great customer service and an incredible product. Add a friendly and well educated staff and you simply can't go wrong. We hand pick all of our flowers to ensure the highest quality product.


While many are flown in from all over the world, we are also located just around the corner from local growers and look forward to showing off the best of what's in season. Fresh cut from the field and straight to your table...doesn't get much better than that! We are open three hundred and sixty five days a year and are widely known for making the holidays a special treat for our customers. Come and see for yourself. We can't wait to meet you.
